In-Home Care Services
in Alberta

When someone you love needs extra help at home, the right support can make daily life safer, easier, and more comfortable. Ephraim Stat Care provides compassionate in-home care services across Alberta for seniors, adults recovering from surgery, people living with chronic conditions, and families who need dependable caregiver support.

Our caregivers come to your home to provide personalized assistance based on your needs, routines, preferences, and level of independence. Whether you need a few hours of help, overnight care, or ongoing support, we are here to help your loved one remain comfortable at home with dignity and respect.

Personalized Home Care That Puts People First

At Ephraim Stat Care, we believe care should never feel rushed. We take time to understand each person as an individual, not just by their age, diagnosis, or care needs. Our approach is patient-centred, family-focused, and built around trust.

We support clients with daily living, companionship, mobility, personal care, meal assistance, reminders, transportation, and other non-medical home care needs. For families, our in-home care services provide peace of mind knowing that a trained and caring professional is there when support is needed most.

In-Home Care Services We Provide

Our Alberta in-home care services may include:

  • Companionship and emotional support
  • Personal care and hygiene assistance
  • Bathing, dressing, and grooming support
  • Meal preparation and feeding assistance
  • Medication reminders
  • Light housekeeping and laundry
  • Mobility and transfer support
  • Help with errands and appointments
  • Post-surgery and recovery support
  • Respite care for family caregivers
  • Overnight and 24-hour care options
  • Support for chronic conditions
  • Palliative and comfort care support

Every care plan is customized. Some families need short-term help while a loved one recovers. Others need ongoing daily or weekly care to help someone stay safely at home.

Helping Seniors Stay Safe
and Comfortable at Home

Many seniors prefer to remain in their own homes for as long as possible. With the right in-home support, this can often be done safely and comfortably.

Our caregivers can help with everyday tasks that may become difficult over time, including bathing, dressing, cooking, walking, and keeping up with household routines. We also provide companionship, which can help reduce loneliness and support emotional well-being.

For families who are balancing work, caregiving, and other responsibilities, in-home care can provide the extra support needed to keep everyone safe and supported.
